About Us

Welcome to VRLFP, where your needs come first. This is a community for people interested in dating, hookups, friendship, and/or roleplaying in virtual reality. Most of our users play VRChat, but we're not going to stop you from meeting up in other VR games or IRL — we'd love to hear about your experiences dating in other games!

We strive to provide you with a wonderful experience, regardless of what you're looking for — we won’t judge. We hope to provide an easy, comfortable, and powerful interface for all of your browsing needs.

Notice we’re missing a gender, sexuality, or other profile option that you want to use on the site? Email us with the name and a brief description so we can help get you in on all the action!

We care about your input! Here at VRLFP we listen carefully to your comments and requests. Have a suggestion for a feature that is not yet available on the site? Email us about your idea and we’ll see what we can do.

We’d like to thank you for taking interest in VRLFP! Our members’ safety is our top priority and with that in mind we invite you to report any abusive, racist, sexist, derogatory, illegal, or otherwise harmful activity coming from any of the users on the site. Just click “report” on a user’s profile and our moderation team will investigate as soon as possible.
